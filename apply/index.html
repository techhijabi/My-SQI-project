NATIONAL INNOVATION DIPLOMA

Please, read carefully all the instructions documented in this guide.

ELIGIBILITY
Candidates seeking admission into any of the programs are expected to have a minimum of 5 credits at senior secondary school certificate or equivalent (SSCE, NTC, NECO). Any Candidate who does not satisfy the above requirement for admission can still apply.

DURATION OF PROGRAMME
The school runs three years modular programmes, each year of study is terminal and has a cogent, flexible structure and content that equips the trainee with a practicable working skill and the possibility to exist at that level. The school runs diploma programmes on a full-time basis for two years, part-time for three years and express for a year. With a one-year compulsory internship attached to any of the options. For full-time students, the programme structure includes at least, two years of study in college, a supervised industrial work experience scheme (SIWES) followed by another two terms of work in the college. The curricula are in modules of employable skills. The qualifications awarded by the college will be the National Innovation Diploma (NID) which is equivalent to the National Diploma (ND).

PROFESSIONAL DIPLOMA

Eligibility
Anyone is eligible to apply for the professional certificate program at SQI College ICT, be it a young school leaver, an undergraduate, an employed graduate, an unemployed graduate or a professional. It’s open to anyone and everyone, provided you have the passion to learn.

Requirements
There are no special requirements to get enrolled in the professional certificate program.

Mode of application
The mode of application is seamless and stress-free. It’s as simple as, register, make payment, and begin lectures.
